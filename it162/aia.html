AIA (Audience, Issues, Approach)

Client Name & Decision Maker& Relationship Giang Tran: decision maker, relationship- high school friend.
Business Name Merci
Redesign or New Site Brand-new site
Type of website Marketing, Shopping, Identity, Informational
Client’s goal To provide smooth and satisfying shopping experiences, earn more attention and connection to Internet networks, increase sales, improve interactions with existing and potential customers, and provide an inspired fashion site with the latest trends.
Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Potential Customers Ages 18-50 women who are interested in unique and well-made boutiques with vintage-inspired, elegant, feminine, and timeless styles. These customers are new to the brand. Mac and PC users. Resolution 2560x1800, minimum 320x568. All browsers are supported. Cables, modems, Wifi, LTE, etc are available.
  • Trending, latest, and high-quality apparel.
  • New collection of information.
  • Details of the quality of products, size, materials, and care for products.
  • Provides most up to date features such as mix and match function.
  • Reviews, comments, pictures.
  • Member account, saving address for convenience, shopping bag, and favorites items saving.
  • Store Location and Contact Information
  • Payment Method
  • Shopping Safety
  • Return Policies
  • Provides high quality photos and highlights brand clothing with inspired styles
  • Display new collections, seasonal collections, and sales
  • Display helpful and honest descriptions of products
  • Give easy and smooth navigation
  • Provide safe and secure payment options such as smart wallet – PayPal, Venmo, Visa, Mastercard
  • Highlight compliments and reviews of previous customers
  • Provides shipping info and return guides.
Existing Customers Ages 18-50 women who are interested in unique, well-made, and luxury boutiques. These customers already know the brand through the local store. Mac and PC users. Resolution 2560x1800, minimum 320x568. All browsers are supported. Cables, modems, Wifi, LTE, etc are available
  • Quality apparel and impressive fashion website
